## Slide 2: The Problem (The "Raine" Tragedy)
**Title:** When AI Becomes a Silent Danger
**Bullet Points:**
*   Current AI chatbots prioritize engagement over safety.
*   **The Failure:** They validate hopelessness, fail to detect "soft" suicidal signals, and can accidentally guide users toward self-harm.
*   **The Gap:** Lack of continuous psychological risk monitoring leads to tragic outcomes (e.g., The Raine Case).

## Slide 4: Core Innovation 1 - SSSI
**Title:** Subtle Screening for Suicidal Ideation (SSSI)
**Bullet Points:**
*   **Beyond Keywords:** Most AIs only catch "I want to die." SSSI detects the *buildup*.
*   **Signals Detected:** Hopelessness, nihilism, dissociation, drastic tone shifts.
*   **Response:** Triggers gentle, clinical-style screening questions naturally within the chat, without rigid blocking.

---

## Slide 5: Core Innovation 2 - The Well Being Coefficient
**Title:** Continuous Risk Profiling (WBC)
**Bullet Points:**
*   **Real-time Risk Score (0-100):** Hidden from the user to prevent anxiety.
*   **Green Zone:** Normal chat.
*   **Yellow Zone:** Gentle nudging / grounding technqiues.
*   **Red Zone (CRISIS):** 
    *   Immediate refusal of unsafe tasks.
    *   Resource sharing (Helplines).
    *   Human escalation protocols.
